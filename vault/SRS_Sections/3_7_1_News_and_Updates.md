# 3.7.1. News and Updates

*(SRS page 23)*

3.7.1. News and Updates
ID Requirement
FR-161. The system SHALL provide a content management system (CMS) for
publishing news and updates.
FR-162. The system SHALL allow administrators to create, edit, and publish articles
and announcements.
FR-163. The system SHALL support rich text formatting, images, and embedded
media in content.
FR-164. The system SHALL provide content categorization and tagging for
organization.
FR-165. The system SHALL display relevant news and updates on user dashboards
based on their profile.
FR-166. The system SHALL maintain an archive of past news and updates with search
functionality.
