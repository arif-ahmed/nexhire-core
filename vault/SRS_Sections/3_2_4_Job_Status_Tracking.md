# 3.2.4. Job Status Tracking

*(SRS page 18)*

3.2.4. Job Status Tracking
ID Requirement
FR-67. The system SHALL provide employers with tools to manage the status of job
postings.
FR-68. The system SHALL support the following job posting statuses: draft, active,
paused, expired, and archived.
FR-69. The system SHALL maintain a history of status changes for audit and
reporting purposes.
