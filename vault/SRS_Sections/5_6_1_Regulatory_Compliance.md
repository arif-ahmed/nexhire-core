# 5.6.1. Regulatory Compliance

*(SRS page 33)*

5.6.1. Regulatory Compliance
ID Requirement
NFR-118. The system SHALL comply with all applicable Palestinian labor laws and
regulations.
NFR-119. The system SHALL incorporate GDPR principles as best practice for data
protection.
NFR-120. The system SHALL comply with accessibility regulations and standards.
NFR-121. The system SHALL maintain appropriate records for regulatory compliance
and auditing.
NFR-122. The system SHALL implement mechanisms to stay current with changing
regulatory requirements.
