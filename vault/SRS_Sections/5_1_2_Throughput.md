# 5.1.2. Throughput

*(SRS page 27)*

5.1.2. Throughput
ID Requirement
NFR-06. The system SHALL support at least 1,000 concurrent users during normal
operations.
NFR-07. The system SHALL support at least 5,000 concurrent users during peak
periods.
NFR-08. The system SHALL process at least 100 job applications per minute during
peak periods.
NFR-09. The system SHALL support at least 500 new job postings per day.
NFR-10. The system SHALL support at least 1,000 new user registrations per day.
